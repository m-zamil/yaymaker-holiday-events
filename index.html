how it works

Yaymaker’s work holiday parties are 100% customizable, whether you’re opting for virtual holiday party games or something in person. Just tell our event planners what you need, and they’ll piece everything together to make it special (and stress-free).

THE MAIN DETAILS WE NEED ARE:

  • What type of company event you’re planning (e.g., Halloween, Thanksgiving, Christmas etc.)
  • When it is
  • How many people are coming
  • Whether you have remote employees, in-person or both
  • Where your party is (if meeting in person)
  • Which experience or package you’d like
  • Any add-ons or customizations you’d like to include
